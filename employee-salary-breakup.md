Employee Salary Breakup
=======================


Employee CTC => 100%

Salary Brerak ups as:

Basic => 30%

HRA => 30%

Conveyances => 25%

Medical => 10%

Other => 5%


Salary Distribution

(A) Basic Salary + HRA 

(B) Conveyance + Medical + Other 

(C) Employers Contribution for PF (12% of Basic) 

(D) Employees Contribution for PF (12% of Basic) 



Gross Salary = (A) + (B) 

CTC = (A) + (B) + (C) 

Net in Hand = (A) + (B) - (D)
